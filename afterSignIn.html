With India hosting 10 million weddings annually, there's a substantial food waste issue amounting to about US$14 billion in losses. Roughly 10 to 20 percent of the food served at these events goes to waste. So why not we redirect this excess food to those in need could help mitigate food wastage effectively?

Do You Know?

Here are some Statistics shown that inspires us.

Out of every 100 units of food, a mere 30 ended up being wasted, while the remaining 70 were consumed. This means that only 30% of the food was wasted, leaving a significant 70% that was enjoyed and nourished people.

India's 2023 GHI score is 28.7, considered serious according to the GHI Severity of Hunger Scale. This is a slight improvement from its 2015 GHI score of 29.2, also considered serious, and shows considerable improvement relative to its 2000 and 2008 GHI scores of 38.4 and 35.5, respectively.
